Course Description and Course Objectives

Course Outcomes

  • Understanding and applying the principles of object-oriented programming in the construction of robust, maintainable programs.
  • Analyze the various societal and environmental problems critically to develop solutions using the features of programming language.
  • Develop sustainable and innovative solutions for real-time problems.
